People's Choice

If your team is interested in entering your project for the People's Choice Award, your team should select so on the Submission Form! Everyone will vote for their favorite projects until there is a clear winner (51% of votes!). Voting will begin at 12 PM EST and end at 3 PM EST at http://tinyurl.com/ii2020vote.

Judging:

The same meeting link will be used for judging throughout the day. Everyone is welcome to attend presentations to watch, but please remain respectful. Your teams will be contacted via Slack 10 minutes to que, and you should be on the Zoom meeting at least 5 minutes prior to your presentation.

The United Engineers host will screenshare the presentation you shared in your submission. Your team leader will be provided remote control to navigate your presentation. Each team has 5 to 7 minutes to present. Up to 3 minutes of follow up questions will be asked by the judges only. Due to time constraints, we will enforce timing strictly.

Feedback

You were the first to participate in this pilot United Engineers event, so your feedback is essential in determining if and how we host more and larger ideathons!
