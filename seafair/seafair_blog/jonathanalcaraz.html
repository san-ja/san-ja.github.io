Jonathan Alcaraz

Philippines | Deck cadet

Jonathan is an aspiring-marine-biologist-turned-seasoned-seafarer. Having been on more than 20 trips in the Philippines already, Jonathan was preparing to embark on his first international trip when the pandemic struck and locked him in Manila.

At sea, Jonathan is a dedicated seafarer on deck. He courageously takes the red-eye navigational watch to prevent any collisions with other boats. In his closest call at sea, Jonathan explains how important watchstanding is - even while in harbor. Yet sometimes, even a watchstander cannot win against the strong currents and big waves which bring ships twice as large as the one Jonathan sailed on only a hair away from collision.

Despite some nerve-wrecking experiences, Jonathan enjoys his time at sea. From catching fish to storytelling with crew members, Jonathan looks on the bright side. In fact, one of his favorite destinations became a favorite while waiting out a storm. During the calm before a storm in anchorage near the island of Samar, Jonathan and his crew found the four days of fishing and swimming to be a well-deserved and memorable break.

From his various adventures, Jonathan hopes seafarers take away one thing: a lot can go wrong, so don’t be cocky and absorb any advice the senior seafarers give like a sponge.

Jonathan, we hope that you get home safely and that you soon have the opportunity to make the long-awaited international trip to Hawaii!
