Ryan Bernardo

Philippines | OS

Ryan is a born seafarer. Currently an OS with a bachelors in marine transportation under his belt, he is working on gaining more seagoing experience before becoming an officer. It is his dream to be a seafarer, but Ryan admits that the process of becoming one is difficult. The hardest part for him was attending strenuous exercises for his STCW certification and completing his apprenticeship.

As a seafarer now, Ryan and his partner enjoyed homemade hamburgers and festivities at home for Day of the Seafarer. He acknowledges that the life of a seafarer is difficult: from spotting pirate vessels on the horizon to sailing through high tides. However, he advises aspiring seafarers to never give up and to stay positive, as the journey is worth it.

On a recent trip to Scotland, Ryan was able to spot some famous castles. Next, he wants to travel to Australia and Japan. Eventually, Ryan would like to be captain.

We know that Ryan looks forward to playing cards and sharing stories with crewmates aboard his next ship.

Ryan -- welcome to the Seafair family! We can’t wait to help you achieve your goals.
