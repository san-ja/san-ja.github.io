Chad Uy

Philippines | Wiper

Chad loves his profession as engine cadet. He enjoys working on maintaining the engine and making friends with crew mates. Chad has wanted to be a seafarer since he was a toddler - it is his dream to travel around the world. Although Chad first faced homesickness and has lived through a super typhoon while at sea, he has already sailed for a total of 32 months and has no plans to stop anytime soon. In the future, Chad hopes to learn more about marine engineering on international vessels, advance into an engineering officer position, and to experience the challenging port of Panama.

Chad is proud to have obtained his license for an engineering officer. He advises incoming seafarers to adopt the same mindset which was required of him to become a successful Filipino seafarer: never stop dreaming, be proactive on board, and ask questions.

In contrast to Chad’s hardworking nature, Chad provided some insight into his highly treasured moments with crew members: watching movies, fishing and swimming while the ship is at port, bean day Fridays… Every day with Chad is filled with learning and enjoyment.

We are proud to have hard workers like Chad in our Seafair network, and we look forward to accompanying them on their future journeys!
